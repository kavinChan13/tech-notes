01是什么 / 为什么

把 未来 90 天 拆成 3 个阶段：30 天 Learn / 60 天 Contribute / 90 天 Lead。每个阶段有可验证的产出。本质上是一个 "短期 IDP + 早期信号检测"。

90 天是公认的"试用期 / 关键观察期" — 不要在 Day 89 才发现不合适。

**Role:** Senior Software Engineer · <Team>
**Start date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Manager:** <Manager>
**Onboarding buddy:** <Peer name>

---

## 🎯 Vision (your 90-day success looks like)
<1 paragraph: by Day 90, what should this person be doing independently>

---

## Day 1-30 · LEARN (吸收)
**Goal:** 理解团队 / 产品 / 工具链，建立人脉

### Weekly milestones
- W1: 入职 setup 完成（access, IDE, build），见过 5 个 team member
- W2: 完成 R&D 培训 (mandatory) + onboarding doc 通读
- W3: 跟着 buddy 完成第 1 个 small PR (≤200 行，已有 mentor)
- W4: 独立完成第 2 个 small PR + 站会能讲清楚自己做了什么

### Deliverables
- [ ] 看完团队所有 design docs (~10 篇)
- [ ] 完成 2 个 small PR
- [ ] 输出"onboarding 反馈 doc"（你以外视角看团队的盲点 = 黄金洞察）
- [ ] 1on1 频次：每周 1 次 (45 min)

### Manager-side commitments
- 提供 reading list + buddy assignment
- 每周 1on1 + 1 次随机午餐
- Day 30 check-in: 双向匹配度对话

---

## Day 31-60 · CONTRIBUTE (贡献)
**Goal:** 独立交付，开始有自己的判断

### Weekly milestones
- W5-6: Owner 一个 medium feature (1-2 wk effort)
- W7: 参加 design review 并 push back 至少 1 次
- W8: 完成 1 次跨团队对接 (e.g. 和 PM/test/BB-team)

### Deliverables
- [ ] Owner 1 个 medium feature 并独立 ship
- [ ] 主持 1 次 small design review
- [ ] 给至少 1 个同事提供 thoughtful code review (非 nit-picking)

### Manager-side commitments
- 安排 1 次 cross-team intro
- 给 specific 反馈（不只是 "you're doing great"）
- Day 60 check-in: 是否需要调整方向

---

## Day 61-90 · LEAD (引领)
**Goal:** 成为团队"靠得住的人"，开始有不可替代性

### Weekly milestones
- W9-10: Owner 一个 larger initiative (multi-week)
- W11: 主导一次技术决策（design 选型 / refactor proposal）
- W12: Day 90 retrospective + 制定下一个 6 个月 IDP

### Deliverables
- [ ] Ship 1 个 visible deliverable to stakeholders outside team
- [ ] 写 1 篇 design doc 被采纳
- [ ] 完成"onboarding 反馈 doc"的 final 版

### Day 90 graduation criteria
- [ ] 团队 3 个不同的人愿意继续和他/她合作
- [ ] 能独立做 small-to-medium 技术决策
- [ ] manager 对未来 6 个月有 clear IDP

---

## Risk indicators (manager should escalate early if)
- Day 30 没完成基本 access / 第 1 个 PR
- Day 60 仍需 hand-holding 完成 small feature
- 反馈"没归属感" / 1on1 越来越短
- Buddy 反映"沟通有困难"

变体 C · EM 接新团队首 90 天

心法 参考 Michael Watkins《The First 90 Days》。核心："Listen, don't break things" — 30 天不要大动作，60 天才形成判断，90 天才推关键改变。
MARKDOWN新 EM 90 天
# <Your Name> · First 90 Days as EM of <Team>

**Start date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**My manager:** <Manager>
**Team size:** N people (direct: M, indirect: K)
**Team context:** <new team / inherited / turnaround / startup>

---

## 🎯 Mission (1 paragraph)
<What does success look like for me by Day 90.
Not "be a great manager" — what will be measurably different.>

---

## Day 1-30 · LISTEN (倾听，don't break things)
**Theme:** 学习，不要急着改

### Must-do
- 1on1 with every direct report (45 min · structured set of 6 questions, see below)
- 1on1 with my manager 2x: alignment on expectations
- 1on1 with 5 key cross-team partners (peer EMs, key PM, lead architect)
- Read all team artifacts: roadmap, OKRs, last 3 QBRs, postmortems, on-call schedule
- Shadow on-call rotation for 1 week
- Attend (not lead) all team rituals (standup / retro / planning)

### 6 questions to ask every direct report (1on1 #1)
1. What would you keep about how the team works today?
2. What would you change tomorrow if you could?
3. What's something my predecessor did well / poorly?
4. What do you need from me to do your best work?
5. If I make one mistake in my first 90 days, what would you bet it is?
6. What aren't I asking that I should?

### Anti-goals (things NOT to do)
- ❌ Announce a reorg
- ❌ Cancel any existing ritual or process
- ❌ Promise specific changes
- ❌ Compare to previous team / company

### Output: SWOT memo (Day 30)
A 1-page memo to my manager: Strengths / Weaknesses / Opportunities / Threats of the team.

---

## Day 31-60 · DIAGNOSE (诊断 + 早期 win)
**Theme:** 形成判断 + 拿一个 quick win

### Must-do
- Identify top 3 problems worth solving (based on Day 30 listening)
- Pick 1 "quick win" (something the team has been asking for, you can deliver in <2 weeks)
- Define your point of view on team's strategy (write it down)
- Start adjusting only obvious dysfunction (broken meetings, missing 1on1s, etc.)

### Output: 60-day plan memo
- Team strengths to preserve
- Top 3 problems with proposed approach
- Quick win delivered (or in progress)
- 90-day OKR draft

---

## Day 61-90 · ACT (开始动)
**Theme:** 推关键 1-2 个改变 + 建立长期节奏

### Must-do
- Drive 1-2 substantive changes (process / structure / talent)
- Establish long-term cadence (weekly update, monthly skip-level, quarterly QBR)
- Make 1 hiring or talent decision (promote / move / hire / part ways)
- Communicate the "why" behind every change

### Output: 90-day review with manager
- What I've learned
- What I've changed (and why)
- What I'll change in next 90 days
- Where I need my manager's support

---

## Personal pitfalls I want to avoid (specific to me)
<1-2 honest observations about your own tendencies>
e.g. "I tend to over-listen and under-decide. Force myself to make 1 visible call by Day 45."
e.g. "I tend to compare with previous team. Catch myself and stop."

⚖️DO / DON'T（3 变体共性铁律）

DO

  • 所有里程碑都是 "可验证产出"，不是"努力"或"学习"
  • 每 30 天必有 正式 check-in，不是 Day 89 才回头看
  • PIP 模板必须 HRBP 参与起草，不是 EM 单独搞
  • 新 EM 模板严格遵守 "30 天不大动作" — 这是 EM 最大的智慧
  • 所有版本都写明 "哪些情况会触发升级 / 失败"

DON'T

  • 新人 plan 写得像 PIP（充满"必须达到"）— 会吓跑好苗子
  • PIP 写得像 onboarding（充满"加油"）— 法律风险，HR 会打回
  • 新 EM 90 天就 reorg — 90% 是灾难
  • 目标含糊（"提升团队效率"）— 必须有数字 / 时间 / 验证人
  • 写完归档，不真的用它做 check-in
